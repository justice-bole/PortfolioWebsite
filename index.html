About Me

A little about myself

My name's Justice; I'm a Software Engineer that creates games and applications using real-time 3D creation tools like Unity and Unreal Engine. I believe that games and virtual simulations have the capability to provide experiences unlike any other medium. Whether your completely immersed in a games story and world building, or using augmented/virtual reality to practice using a complex piece of machinery, digital technology has the power to shape our world. My passion is digital technology, but my love is my family and daughter. When I'm not working on my next big project, I'm spending time with my family, gaming, cooking, or creating 3D art.
